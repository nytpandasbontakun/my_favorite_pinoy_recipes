Pork Sinigang

is a Filipino soup or stew characterized by its sour and savoury taste. It is most often associated with tamarind, although it can use other sour fruits and leaves as the souring agent. It is one of the more popular dishes in Filipino cuisine.

-Wikipedia

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg Pork (cut into cubes)
  • Knorr Sinigang Mix
  • Tomatoes (about a cup)
  • Kangkong or Spinach
  • 1 Onion (cut in quarters)
  • 1 liter water

Instructions:

  1. In a pot, Saute pork until slightly brown.
  2. Add water onion and tomatoes then let it boil
  3. Add sinigang mix then let it boil again until pork is soft
  4. Add kangkong or spinach then let it cook for a minute or two
  5. Serve and Enjoy!
